UI Experience

This experience is a concept to show how the Environment Reader would help someone who is blind or visually impaired navigate virtual reality. While we can't fully render VR here, we can give an idea of how AI can assist in an immersive, ever-changing UI.

This simulation demonstrates the AI agent of the Environment Reader assisting with tasks via a chat interface, or helping in apps you already use, like a messenger app.
