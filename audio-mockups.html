AI Mockup Recordings

This page contains recordings of how the AI portion of the environment reader would be expected to respond to user requests. The recordings demonstrate myself, Ethan, asking questions to Gemini Live. Gemini Live is acting as an assistant within a virtual reality environment.

Using the AI assistant to navigate your apps

This recording contains the following:

  • The AI describing the list of apps.
  • The AI opens a game on the user's screen.
  • The AI assists the user with getting into position to play the game.

Going to user environments or virtual reality environments

This recording demonstrates the following:

  • The AI agent guides the user to a virtual concert.
  • The AI describes the crowd and venue.
  • AI describes the scenery of the concert: the stage lights coming up, and the virtual avatar performing on the stage.
  • The user then asks to be teleported to a friend's campfire.
