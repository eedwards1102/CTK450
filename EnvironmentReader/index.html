The Environment Reader

Welcome to the environment reader project.

Welcome to the environment reader project, pushing screen readers to read the room, literally. The environment reader project aims to create a VR screen reader that is made for 3D environments. Traditional screen readers are designed mainly for 2D navigation on devices like smartphones and tablets. While this design language works for flat surfaces, it breaks the immersive feel of VR and makes navigation difficult. The environment reader blends AI, spatial audio, and haptics to assist a user in navigating VR intuitively.

How the Environment Reader Works

Audio and Haptics

  • Spatial Audio: Uses audio icons for 3D grid exploration; allows users to locate objects in space.
  • Haptics: Provide a tactile layer for objects; makes navigation feel realistic.
  • Audio and haptics empower users to navigate VR on their own terms.
